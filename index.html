Test Protocol

  1. Start with 1000 Hz
  2. Begin at 30 dB HL
  3. If patient responds, decrease by 10 dB until no response
  4. Increase by 5 dB until response (threshold)
  5. Test 1500, 2000, 3000, 4000, 6000, 8000, then 500, 250 Hz
  6. Test opposite ear
